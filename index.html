Food Dispenser

The food dispenser works on the same principle with the water dispenser and composes of 2 parts that are main food depot and the food bowl. The sensor under the food bowl continuously measures the dry food level in the vessel. When the amount of food in the container drops below the specified values, the microcontroller runs the pump motor for a while and transfers food main depot to the food bowl.

Water Dispanser

The water dispenser compose of 2 parts. First part is the main water tank and the second one is the bowl that cats can drink water. There is a sensor under the water bowl that measures the liquid level in the bowl and when the amount of water in the container drops below the specified values, the microcontroller runs the pump motor for a while and transfers it from the water in the main tank to the vessel. During the operation of the pump motor, the microcontroller constantly checks the water level and stops the motor when it reaches the requested level. In this way, a certain amount of water in the water container always remains at a drinkable level.

Heater

There is a heater and a heat sensor inside Cat-Box. The microcontroller measures the internal temperature of the cat house. When the values obtained as a result of the measurements fall below the specified minimum temperature value, a microcontroller activates the heater to increase the ambient temperature by means of a relay. During the time the heater is running, the measurements continues and when the desired minimum temperature is reached, the microcontroller turns on the relay to switch off the heater.

Mobil App

Microcontroller controls the values for water / food amount and heat and makes the necessary actions autonomously. In addition, it uses the WiFi module to transmit information Design these values and actions taken, to a server located on the internet. With this data transmitted, the user can monitor the status of liquid / food in the Cat-Box and display various statistics Design them via the Web application. This information is sent to the Web application as HTTP Request by using a web service. Each information can be saved in the database and various reports can be generated via this information.

Scope

Cat-box is an Arduino-based cat house designed for people who would like to own a cat and cannot feed them at home. It is a house where domestic cats can live outside the house and all of their water, food and heating needs can be met remotely through an application. The inner temperature and the amount of food or water in the containers can be checked via the mobile application / Catbox website. In addition, the pet owners will be able to keep track of the record of their feeding history, so that they can investigate the cause if there is any difference other than their normal behavior.

What are we doing?

The purpose of this project is to bring together both the people who would like to own a cat but cannot feed them inside the home and the domestic cats that can live outside the house as comfortable as inside. This will be only possible with the box outside their home that includes all the requirements of cats to live comfortably. That is the reason why this project is appeared.
